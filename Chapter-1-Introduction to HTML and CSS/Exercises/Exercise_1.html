Most Delecious Fudgy Brownies Recipe

These ultimate chocolate brownies are fudgy, rich and absolutely delecious! Perfect for chocolate lovers, these brownies have a crisp top and a gooey, moist center that melts in your mouth.

Recipe Details

Serving size: 9-12 brownies

Prep time: 20 minutes

Cook time: 25-30 minutes

You will need:

Method/Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a square baking pan
  2. Melt butter in a large microwave-safe bowl in the microwave for some seconds
  3. Add in sugar to the butter until well combined
  4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well
  5. Add in vanilla extract
  6. In a separate bowl, mix the dry ingridients (cocoa, flour, salt and baking powder)
  7. Gradually add dry ingredients to wet mixture
  8. Add in the chocolate chips
  9. Pour batter into prepared pan and bake for 25-30 minutes
  10. Let cool before cutting into squares
